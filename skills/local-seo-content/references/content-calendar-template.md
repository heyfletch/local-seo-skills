# Content Calendar Template

## Recommended Cadence
- New site: 2-4 blog posts/month (build content base)
- Established site: 1-2 blog posts/month (maintain + refresh)
- GBP posts: 2-3 per week
- Social: Platform-dependent

## Blog Calendar Format

```markdown
# Content Calendar: [Business Name] — [Month/Quarter]

## Blog Posts

| Week | Topic | Target Keyword | Type | Word Count | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Topic] | [keyword] | [educational/commercial] | [count] | [high/med/low] |
| 2 | [Topic] | [keyword] | [type] | [count] | [priority] |
```
